## Project Overview

The National Budget Transparency Portal is a public-facing platform that transforms Ghana's national budget from a dense government document into a clear, searchable, and visually accessible resource for citizens, journalists, and researchers.

It ingests official budget data — allocations, expenditure releases, and actual spending — to make fiscal transparency a practical reality.

## Core Features

- **Budget Overview Dashboard**: High-level summary of revenue, expenditure, and deficit/surplus positions at a glance.
- **Sector Allocation Explorer**: Deep-dives into Education, Health, Agriculture, and Infrastructure funding with international benchmarking.
- **Ministry & Agency Explorer**: Searchable directory of budget profiles and execution rates for all spending entities.
- **Geographic Budget Map**: Mapping sub-national fiscal data, including regional development funds and infrastructure project locations.
- **Expenditure Tracker**: Tracking actual spending versus allocations to identify under-execution and oversight gaps.
- **Revenue Tracker**: Monitoring tax collection performance and trends against national targets.
- **Public Debt Monitor**: Making Ghana's domestic and external debt position accessible and understandable.

## Impact

By making budget data accessible, the portal improves oversight, reduces accountability deficits, and empowers informed advocacy and journalism in public finance management.
