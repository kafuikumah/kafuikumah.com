## Project Overview

The COVID-19 Dashboard is a real-time public health intelligence platform built to give Ghanaians — citizens, healthcare workers, government officials, and institutions — a clear, continuously updated picture of the pandemic's trajectory across the country.

Where official health communications have historically relied on press briefings, PDF reports, and static weekly summaries, this platform makes pandemic data live, geographic, and interactive.

## Core Features

- **National Situation Summary**: A single-screen headline view displaying metrics like total confirmed cases, new cases, deaths, and vaccination progress, updated daily.
- **Regional Outbreak Map**: Interactive choropleth map of Ghana's 16 regions, color-coded by outbreak intensity with district-level drill-downs.
- **Vaccination Progress Tracker**: Granular tracking of first, second, and booster dose coverage against national targets.
- **Healthcare System Capacity Monitor**: Real-time visibility into ICU bed occupancy, ventilator availability, and treatment center capacity.
- **Epidemiological Trend Analysis**: Deep analytical layer for professionals, including Rt estimates and epidemic curve wavy wave annotations.
- **Variant Surveillance Panel**: Tracking genomic sequencing results to monitor variant dominance and emergence.

## Impact

The platform closes the information asymmetry gap, enabling faster decision-making, better resource allocation (ICU beds, etc.), and building public trust through transparency.
