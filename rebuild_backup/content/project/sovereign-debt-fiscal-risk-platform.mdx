## Project Overview

The Sovereign Debt & Fiscal Risk Intelligence Platform is a financial reporting and analytics system that visualizes Ghana's public debt portfolio, economic indicators, and fiscal risk projections.

It is designed for real-time risk management by the Ministry of Finance and for enhanced parliamentary and public oversight of the country's fiscal future.

## Core Features

- **National Debt Overview**: Clear summary of total public debt (domestic and external) as absolute figures and % of GDP.
- **Debt Portfolio Explorer**: Detailed breakdown by creditor class (Bilateral, Multilateral, Commercial), currency, and maturity.
- **Debt Service Profile**: Visualization of future debt service obligations for the next 20 years, identifying peak service years.
- **Fiscal Performance Monitor**: Tracking revenue performance, expenditure execution, and primary balance trends.
- **Debt Sustainability Analysis (DSA)**: Accessible dashboards showing risk classification and stress test scenarios (growth shocks, exchange rate depreciation).
- **Economic Context**: Real-time tracking of GDP growth, inflation, exchange rates, and foreign exchange reserves.
- **IMF Program Tracker**: Monitoring milestones, benchmarks, and disbursement schedules under the ECF program.

## Impact

The platform builds fiscal credibility through transparency, providing the warning signs needed to prevent debt crises and supporting Ghana's return to international capital markets.
