## Project Overview

The SDG Performance Tracker is a development monitoring platform that measures Ghana's progress across the UN Sustainable Development Goals — tracking 232 indicators and translating them into strategic intelligence.

It serves both for external accountability with international partners and internal decision-making to guide sector investments.

## Core Features

- **Goal-Level Deep Dives**: Dedicated analytical sections for all 17 goals, showing current values, baselines, and 2030 targets.
- **Geographic Disaggregation**: Regional SDG maps to identify disparities and ensure "leaving no one behind" is measurable.
- **Equity Analysis**: Disaggregating progress by sex, age, wealth quintile, and urban/rural location.
- **Investment Gap Analysis**: Connecting indicator performance to investment needs and identifying private vs. public sector potential.
- **VNR Support Module**: Automating the data backbone for Ghana's Voluntary National Reviews to the United Nations.
- **Data Quality Monitor**: Transparency about data methodology, reference years, and next update dates.

## Impact

The tracker acts as a national learning system, helping Ghana identify what works in its development agenda and positioning the country to access development finance through demonstrated impact.
