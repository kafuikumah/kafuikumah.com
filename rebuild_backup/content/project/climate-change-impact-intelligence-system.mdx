## Project Overview

The Climate Change Impact Intelligence System is a national-scale environmental monitoring platform that consolidates climate data, environmental risk indicators, emissions tracking, and sustainability progress into a single, continuously updated intelligence dashboard.

It serves as decision infrastructure for climate adaptation and mitigation, tracking exposure to environmental risk, emissions trends, and national climate commitments.

## Core Features

- **National Climate Risk Monitor**: Real-time overview of temperature anomalies, rainfall deviations, and drought severity across the country.
- **Emissions & Carbon Tracking**: Monitoring GHG profiles against national commitments (NDCs) and tracking deforestation via satellite alerts.
- **Environmental Health Indicators**: Tracking air quality, water body health, and land degradation indices.
- **Agricultural Climate Impact Tracker**: Connecting climate conditions to food security with seasonal crop stress indices and yield forecast deviations.
- **Sustainability Progress Tracker**: Monitoring progress against the Paris Agreement, SDG 13, and national environmental policies.
- **Climate Projection Explorer**: Forward-looking tool for 20, 30, and 50-year climate projections by region.

## Impact

The system makes Ghana's international climate commitments monitorable and accountability enforceable, while providing the analytical foundation to access global climate finance.
